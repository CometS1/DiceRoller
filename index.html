This is a simple dice rolling web page I created as a test. Choose the dice you want to roll based on the type of die (a die with 6 faces can roll 1 to 6, etc.), choose the amount of dice to roll, the amount (modifier) to add to the roll, then click roll and get the results. Feel free to use it for any gaming purposes you may have.
